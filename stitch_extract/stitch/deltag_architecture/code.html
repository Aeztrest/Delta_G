System Limitations

Simulate ≠ Execution

Simulations occur in a static snapshot. Competitive block state changes may result in divergent final execution results.

Account Limits

Highly complex transactions exceeding the maximum pre-fetch account limit (currently 64) will be rejected by the analyze pipeline.

x402 Optionality

Wallet flow for x402 is optional. Users may choose direct payment or credit-based models depending on protocol tier.
